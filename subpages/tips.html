Maintenance Tips

Here we give do's and dont's tips ensuring safety and longevity of your garage door.

Do's

  • In order to keep the door running smoothly we recommend lubricating moving parts with white lithium or silicone lubricants. Wd-40 is ok in a pinch of either if the other two are unavailable.(Torsion Springs, rollers/pulleys, drums and hinges)
  • It's good practice to check the torsion or extension springs by lowering the door half way. If the door stays then it's balanced but if it doesn't then the springs may need an adjustment or replacement. With an opener you have to pull the center string to release lock to operate manually.
  • If you see a broken spring call your local service proffessional.
  • Make sure nothing is blocking the operation of the door or in the path of the safety-i beams.
  • Make sure to keep remotes in secure locations , such as a locked vehicle or house.
  • If you hear any types of noises or the door is not able to open and close, remove the vehicle if applicable. Close the door or leave it open until inspection has been completed. Call us to help you.

Dont's

  • Do not lubricate extension springs as it's not needed and heavy grease's are not recommended.
  • Never allow children or adults to ride and or hang on the door during operation. Garage doors are not meant for this purpose which if ignored can cause serious injury.
  • Caution: Please do not remove any door hinges especially the bottom at anypoint in time. Let a qualified tech service the door to avoid any type of injuries.
  • Springs can be under high loads of tension and can be very dangerous if not serviced properly. Do not try to fix springs or parts under load. Call us to send a qualified professional to fix your door.
  • Refrain from putting hands near tracks while door is in operation.
  • If your door has extension springs with no safety cables present that poses a safety hazard and due to code changes they are required. You shouldn't operate your door without them.
  • If your opener doesn't have safety-i beams you are doing yourself a huge dis-service by not having them especially with safety aspects. Contact us to replace your opener with a safer model.
